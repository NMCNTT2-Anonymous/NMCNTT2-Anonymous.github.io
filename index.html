About Us

Our team was established on February 27th , 2017. We teamed up to complete a project for Introduction to IT 2. Our team has four members, who all come from Talented Class 16, Ho Chi Minh University of Science.

In this project, we'll tackle traffic problems, especially traffic accidents, a common problem in Vietnam by introducing a smart helmet with cutting edge technologies.

Our Product

Our product is a smart helmet whose main purpose is to reduce traffic accidents by offering many traffic supportive features. Besides, we also bring a whole bunch of features that make biking easier than ever

  • MBH++

    Best Helmet Ever
Auto Signal System

Automatically send SOS messages to relatives' phones when fatal impacts occur.

  • MBH++

    Best Helmet Ever
Smart Warning

Measuring speed and location to warn rider if they exceed the safety limit.

  • MBH++

    Best Helmet Ever
Traffic Helper

Connect to your smartphone, and you will have a virtual assitant who can help you find routes, avoid traffic congestion, tell you the time, and so much more.

  • MBH++

    Best Helmet Ever
Hand Free Calling & Reading Messages

No more need to get your phone out whenever you have incoming calls or messages, or you want to call someone, with our helmet you can handle those tasks with ease.

  • MBH++

    Best Helmet Ever
Integrated Dash Camera

Use micro SD card for recording last 5 minutes of journeys, which is invaluable when an accident happens.

  • MBH++

    Best Helmet Ever
Group Control

You hate to travel with a group and then everyone has their own routes? This helmet will connect your group together and when someone gets lost or has some problems, you will get the notifications.
